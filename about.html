    Terence Zhu

    Hey, you’ve come across Terence Zhu [or Nememantis], an illustrator and storyboard artist in Vancouver, BC. I dream and draw mechs and superheroes, and collect figures [3 shelves full so far]. I believe that the best ideas come out of chaotic idea sprouting and careful selection.

    I can work in pre-production phases from conceptual visual development to style framing and storyboarding. You can see what I’ve been up to here at https://www.behance.net/nememantis or my personal site at https://nememantis.art/.

    If you have a project that might benefit from my chaos and craft, let’s connect. Can’t wait to see what we’ll create.
